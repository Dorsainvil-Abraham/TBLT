About TBLT

Welcome to That Little Black Thing: The Ultimate Destination for the Perfect Black Dress


At That Little Black Thing, we believe that every woman deserves to feel effortlessly chic, confident, and timeless in her style. And what better way to capture this essence than with the iconic little black dress? Our mission is simple: to curate the best collection of black dresses for every woman, every body, and every occasion. From casual daytime looks to elegant evening wear, our dresses are designed to make you feel powerful and beautiful, no matter the moment.


Our team handpicks each dress to ensure a diverse range of cuts, lengths, and styles—whether you're seeking a classic, elegant look or something more modern and edgy. At That Little Black Thing, we focus exclusively on black dresses because we understand the versatility and timelessness this wardrobe essential offers. We're here to simplify your shopping experience by providing endless options for the perfect black dress.


Our Promise


We are committed to quality, fit, and style, ensuring that each dress meets our standards before it reaches you. Whether you're shopping for a black dress for work, a party, or a special event, we have something for every occasion, all in one place. Let us help you find your next little black thing!
